🎼 Private Instruction
  • Private lessons are currently offered in Guitar, Bass, Keyboards, Drums, Brass, Woodwinds, Strings, and Voice.
  • Available in 1/2 hour, or 1 hour periods, and conveniantly scheduled on the same day at the same times each week.
  • We gladly offer Family and Senior Citizen discounts.
🎼 Group Lessons
  • Group lessons are offered in Keyboard, Drum and Guitar. Geared toward the beginner, they meet once per week, for 8 weeks.
  • Group classes create a solid foundation for the student in their chosen instrument.
  • The perfect choice for the first-time musician who is not ready to make the commitment to private lessons.
  • Our spacious Group Lesson Rooms are well equipped, comfortable, and can accomodated groups of all sizes.
🎼 Build A Band Program
  • A one week opportunity for young musician to gain experience working, performing and recording in an ensemble. (5 days, 3 hours per day)
  • Work with instructors privately on technique, timing, scales, chords, arpeggios, and intervals to build sequences and emulate or create solos.
  • Work with other musicians in groups, arrange and put together songs into a finished product, and finally record a CD of the band's performance.
  • Instructive seminars on: electronics, amp and pedal set-up & adjustment, guitar and bass tuning, adjusting, cleaning, and general repair.
🎼 Musicians of Tomorrow
  • This program exposes young children to many different types of instruments such as: guitar, keyboards, drums & percussion.
  • Children will experience many differnt sounds and rhythms, which can help them later when choosing an instrument for school.
  • There will be arts and crafts that consist of drawing, coloring, and making many types of musical crafts.
🎼 Music for Seniors
  • This program will let seniors experience playing many different kinds of musical instruments.
  • They will be able to gain a better understanding of different types of music, such as: swing, jazz, rock, country and blues.
  • There will also be crafts and a lunch option offered aswell.
